Educational philosophy

Constructivist approach

I believe learning is an active, contextualized process. My methods focus on inquiry, real-world problem solving, and reflective practice — building knowledge collaboratively.

Inclusive by design

Universal Design for Learning (UDL) guides every course and resource I create. Accessibility, multiple means of engagement, and representation are non-negotiable.

Education is not the filling of a pail, but the lighting of a fire. I design for curiosity, agency, and lasting impact.
